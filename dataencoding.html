Data Encoding Table

Component Type Number of bits/range Description/Comments
Where? ASCII 120 bits Names can be long, so we need enough space to make the name recognizable, at the very least.
Body of water? ASCII 72 bits Only determines the body of water. Most common names for bodies of water are under 9 characters.
What is seen in the water? ASCII 360 bits Need many bits to convey several chunks of information, as they could see many things in the water, or nothing at all. Includes space for commas and spaces.
What kind of animals did you see? ASCII 360 bits Another list that can have many answers. Animals can have a wide range, depending on where the person is.
How many did you see? Number 5 bits Rough estimate. Can't be too big of a number.
What kinds of plants? ASCII 360 bits General plant life terms. Spaces and commas taken into account as well.
